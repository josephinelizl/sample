STORY

The facts are that Surge Coffee Bar & Billiards sits on 6200 square feet in the Albany Park area. It has 14 professional pooltables, a 20 person U-shaped bar and a contemporary atmosphere. Sprinkle a full coffee shop every morning and a full menu in the evening....and that rounds off all the facts of Surge. But there is more to it than facts.Surge Coffee Bar & Billiards came from our want to bring a social hub and a place of community to Albany Park, similar to our other location Pressure Billiards in Edgewater. Centered around a recreational sport, Surge aims to be a place where the diversity of the area is represented in the diversity of the services we offer. Come work, shoot pool, have drinks with friends or sip coffee…just know you’re welcome!

“BRINGING COMMUNITIES TOGETHER, PLAYFULLY”

WHAT WE DO/WHY WE DO IT

COFFEE

Good morning. Rise and Shine. Wake up. Coffee is a necessity in the lives of many. Great coffee is for those lucky enough to find it. Enter Metropolis Coffee Company …locally roasted and brewed daily at Surge. Whether you are looking for that morning latte or afternoon iced coffee, we have you covered. As an added bonus, our house blend is a dark roast from Aspire Coffeeworks. Every cup of that goes towards a good cause!

BAR

Meet for drinks. Catch a game. Grab a bite. Keeping it local, you’ll find liquors in our well from ch distilleryy and drafts from breweries around the region. While socializing on our 20-seat, U-shaped bar, be sure to try our hand-crafted house cocktails. If your choice of drink is wine, beer, cider or a martini, we have you covered. Looking forward to seeing you at the bar.

POOL

A sea of blue…tables that is. 14, 9ft Brunswick Gold Crown IVs scattered in 4,000 square feet of space, surrounded by a contemporary atmosphere. Tables good enough for those looking to work on their shot and cool enough to bring your date and chill. No skill required at Surge. As long as you enjoy music, drinks and socializing with friends, the pool is just a way to spend time with buds. After all, you miss every shot you don’t take (had to put that in here).
